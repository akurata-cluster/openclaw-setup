# Recommended Skills for OpenClaw

To fully realize OpenClaw's potential within the `akurata-cluster` infrastructure, consider installing and configuring the following skills.

## Core Engineering Capabilities

*   **`github` (Configured):** Essential for automating code review, managing issues, and orchestrating PR deployments.
*   **`coding-agent`:** A powerful skill that allows OpenClaw to write, refactor, and review code natively within your repository. Essential for complex multi-file diffs and scaffolding new AI models.
*   **`healthcheck`:** vital for auditing the security, risk posture, and overall health of the hosts and servers that run your AI workloads.

## Communication & Observability

*   **`slack` or `discord`:** Configure your preferred messaging platform. OpenClaw can act as an intelligent notification hub, filtering out noise and summarizing critical alerts from your infrastructure or GitHub.
*   **`session-logs`:** If your team requires an audit trail of agentic behavior, session logging ensures transparency and traceability.

## Data Ingestion

*   **`web_search` & `web_fetch` (Built-in):** Invaluable for proactive research and pulling in the latest technical documentation or API specifications.
*   **`pdf` (Built-in):** Critical for parsing research papers, whitepapers, and architectural specifications without leaving the workspace.

## Personalization

*   **`weather`:** A simple skill for testing heartbeat scheduling and basic data retrieval capabilities.
